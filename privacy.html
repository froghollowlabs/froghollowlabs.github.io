What Data We Collect

RuckHero collects only what's necessary to track your rucks and calculate your calorie burn:

  • Personal profile data: Body weight, age, height, and sex (entered during onboarding)
  • Location data: GPS coordinates during active ruck sessions (distance, pace, elevation)
  • Session data: Duration, ruck weight, calories burned, route points

Where Your Data Lives

All data stays on your device. RuckHero does not have a backend server, cloud database, or user accounts. Your personal information and ruck history are stored locally on your iPhone and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

If you enable Apple Health integration, RuckHero writes completed workout data to Apple Health on your device. This is controlled entirely by you and Apple's Health privacy settings.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to any server
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal information
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs
  • We do not create user accounts or require email addresses
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera

Location Data

RuckHero uses GPS location only during active ruck sessions to calculate distance, pace, and elevation. Location tracking stops when you end or discard a session. Route data is stored locally on your device as part of your session history.

RuckHero does not track your location in the background when you are not actively rucking.

Apple Health

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, RuckHero writes the following data to Apple Health:

  • Workout type (Hiking)
  • Calories burned
  • Distance
  • Duration

RuckHero does not read data from Apple Health. You can revoke Health access at any time in your iPhone's Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

Third-Party Services

RuckHero does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Delete individual sessions by swiping to delete in the History tab
  • Delete all data by deleting the RuckHero app from your device
  • Reset your profile through the Settings screen

Children's Privacy

RuckHero does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is intended for general fitness tracking use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on our website. The effective date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated.
